About Us

We Are The World's Biggest University

Kadet technological University is a public research University in Houghton, Michigan, founded in 1876 as the Kadet mining school, the first post-secondary institution in the upper peninsuls of Michigan. The university moved to Southfield in 1926 and has since expanded to 405 acres.
